Say hi to your new production-grade document extraction service, turning complex information into LLM-ready inputs. No training required. Our documentation provides an overview of our API endpoints, as well as templates on how to best utilize Pulse STUDIO. 

## Key Capabilities

- **AI-Ready Output**: Structured JSON perfect for LLM training and RAG systems
- **Computer Vision**: Built-in support for diagrams, tables, and complex layouts
- **Multimodal Processing**: Handles text, images, and mixed-content documents

## Supported Documents

Our API successfully processes a wide range of documents:

- **Business Documents**: Contracts, invoices, purchase orders
- **Financial Documents**: Annual reports, balance sheets, bank statements
- **Technical Content**: Research papers, patents, technical specifications
- **Healthcare Records**: Clinical notes, lab reports, medical histories
- **Legal Documents**: Agreements, court filings, regulatory submissions
